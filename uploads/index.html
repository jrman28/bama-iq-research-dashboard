06 — Elevation

Shadows & Depth

Every surface layer has a unique shadow recipe. The light source is always from above. Teal shadows for interactive elements, neutral shadows for containers.

--shadow-video
Video wrapper. Subtle base + deep lift.
--shadow-card
Form card. White top-edge highlight + layered depth.
--shadow-btn
Primary button default. Teal glow.
--shadow-btn-hover
Button hover. Elevated teal glow.
--shadow-btn-active
Button press. Depressed, glow reduced.
--shadow-input / focus
Input default: inset depth. Focus: + teal ring.

10 — Locked

Locked Decisions

These are non-negotiable. Do not reverse without explicit discussion. Each has a reason.

Visual
Light mode only — no dark mode
The dark video creates screen-within-screen contrast that only works on a light background. Dark mode destroys this effect.
Visual
Video background stays #080D14
Intentional contrast — "screen within a screen." Not a mistake. Do not lighten to match page theme.
Visual
Whitespace below video is intentional
Do not fill it. The breathing room anchors the video. Adding content destroys the editorial weight.
Copy
"No spam. Just signal." under form only
Its placement beneath the submit button is deliberate reassurance. It does not belong in the footer or anywhere else.
Copy
Footer shows domain only
alabamaiq.com only — no tagline, no mission statement in the footer.
Component
Signal labels: 01 / 02 / 03 DM Mono
Not dots, not dashes, not arrows. Zero-padded DM Mono numbers only — they're editorial marks, not bullet points.
Component
Questionnaire is always optional
Never make it blocking. Skip button always visible. 600ms delay gives confirmation moment before it appears.
Component
Form card glassmorphism is intentional
Frosted warm cream on cream is different from generic purple AI startup glass. The warm tint (252,248,241) is the key distinction.
Styling
Inline styles are primary
Tailwind is used for layout only. All design-critical styles use inline objects for precision and co-location with component logic.
